# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to **support@cajeer.com**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
